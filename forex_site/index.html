How Does It Work and How to start ?

When you open a brokerage account through us, the brokerage pays us part of their spread or commission profit for every trade you make as
compensation for referring a customer to them. We then share the majority of our revenue with you, paying you a cash rebate for each trade you
make as thank you for signing up with us. Your spreads and trading conditions remain the same as if you had opened an account directly with
the broker. The only difference is: as our client you earn extra cash per trade, making trading through us more profitable than opening direct with
the broker.
