Autism

Autism, or autism spectrum disorder, refers to a range of conditions characterized by challenges with social skills, repetitive behaviors, speech and nonverbal communication, as well as by unique strengths and differences. Families realized that they have to adapt and learn how to raise a child that they can’t communicate with, and that child has to adapt to live in a world that he can’t make connections with. When a child is diagnosed with Autism, his family’s life completely changes. 24h observation to the child is a must, special diet, sometimes the house must be reorganized differently, regular visits to the doctor, a lot of forms must be filled regularly by his teachers, parents, nurse and a lot other procedures and precautions should be take in mind. And here comes our role. Our main objective of this project is to make the life of those families a bit easier. We will build a website that will be completely free, all you need is just an account.
